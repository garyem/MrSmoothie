How to make it
1
Add your milk first — stops everything sticking to the blades.
2
Add oats, banana, peanut butter, yoghurt, and cocoa powder.
3
Throw in chia seeds or protein powder if you're using them.
4
Blend for 45–60 seconds until smooth.
5
Taste it — add honey only if you want it a touch sweeter.
6
Pour into a bottle and you're out the door.
No blender? No problem
🥄 Make it the night before — no equipment needed

All you need is a jar, a fork, and 5 minutes the evening before. The oats soften overnight in the fridge and the banana breaks down naturally into the liquid. By morning it's thick, creamy, and ready to drink.

1
Mash the banana well with a fork in a large jar or container.
2
Add yoghurt, peanut butter, and cocoa powder. Stir until combined.
3
Pour in the milk and stir again. Whisk the cocoa if it clumps.
4
Stir in the oats and chia seeds if using. Add honey to taste.
5
Seal and refrigerate overnight. Shake well before drinking.
Skip these without a blender
Protein powder — clumps badly when stirred. Leave it out and rely on the yoghurt and peanut butter for protein instead.
GaryM's tips

Prep it the night before. Blend everything, seal it in a bottle, refrigerate. Shake in the morning. Fresh for up to 24 hours.

Too thick? Splash in more milk and blend again. Not sweet enough? A teaspoon of honey fixes that fast.
