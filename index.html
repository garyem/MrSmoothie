How to make it
1
Add your milk first — stops everything sticking to the blades.
2
Add oats, banana, peanut butter, yoghurt, and cocoa powder.
3
Throw in chia seeds or protein powder if you're using them.
4
Blend for 45–60 seconds until smooth.
5
Taste it — add honey only if you want it a touch sweeter.
6
Pour into a bottle and you're out the door.
No blender? No problem
🥄 Make it the night before — no equipment needed

All you need is a jar and a fork. The oats soften overnight and the banana breaks down naturally. By morning it's thick, creamy, and ready.

1
Mash the banana well with a fork in a large jar.
2
Add yoghurt, peanut butter, and cocoa. Stir until combined.
3
Pour in the milk and stir. Whisk if cocoa clumps.
4
Stir in the oats and chia seeds if using. Add honey to taste.
5
Seal and refrigerate overnight. Shake well before drinking.
Skip without a blender
Protein powder — clumps badly when stirred. The yoghurt and peanut butter give you plenty of protein anyway.
GaryM's tips

Prep it the night before. Blend, seal, refrigerate. Shake in the morning. Fresh for 24 hours.

Too thick? Splash in more milk. Not sweet enough? A teaspoon of honey fixes it.
